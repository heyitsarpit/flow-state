The v1 wire remains deliberately narrow. A new version is required for durable
facts that cannot be dual-read without changing meaning. Hosts still own
durable storage, process-death recovery, and external I/O idempotency; Flow
State guarantees decode-before-mutation and coherent in-process publication.
